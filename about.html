A brief insight into Medical Hospital

Medical is an healthcare industry established in 2010. Medical is a diversified business leveraging the skills and resources at its disposal across various business units of its operations. The strategic objective and vision of Medical is to provide a total quality healthcare business to cover critical areas of healthcare through its different business units. The owners and shareholders of Medical sees opportunities in the hostile business environments where Medical operates to unleash the natural and acquired skills of its dedicated human resources in the niche areas of Medical’s competence. To this end, Medical sets to provide quality Clinical Services (THE HOSPITAL), Strategic Insight Diagnostics, Strategic Insight Education (Continuing Medical Education and Training), Strategic Insight Medicolegal, Strategic Insight Research, and Strategic Insight Pharmaceuticals & Devices, Strategic Insight Publishing (Speakers’ Bureau and Book/Journal Publishing).
